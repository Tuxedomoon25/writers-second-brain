# Experiences — Your Writing Journey

Episodic memory: every scene you write creates a record here.

## Structure

Each scene gets its own folder:

```
experiences/scenes/act1-ch1-scene1/
├── draft.md            ← The scene as written
├── quality-review.md   ← Quality scores and feedback
├── revisions.md        ← Changes made after review
└── learnings.md        ← Patterns extracted for future use
```

## How It Works

1. `/write-scene` creates the draft and saves it here
2. `/scene-review` adds the quality assessment
3. Revisions are tracked automatically
4. Patterns from learnings flow into `memory/patterns/`

## Why This Matters

Your experiences are searchable. When you face a similar writing challenge, `/recall` can find how you solved it before — complete with quality scores showing what worked.
