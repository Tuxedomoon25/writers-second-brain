# My Writing Influences

## Ernest Hemingway — The Eye

**What I use**: Physical precision. Restraint. The iceberg theory — 90% of the story is below the surface. Emotions shown through action, objects, and the body's response to stress. Never named. Never explained.

**How it shapes my prose**: Short declarative sentences for impact. Specific physical details (the weight of the watch, the scratch of stone on palm, the copper taste of fear). The narrator observes but does not interpret. The reader does the emotional work.

**The rule it generates**: Show Don't Tell (Rule 3), Verb Supremacy (Rule 4), Anti-Abstraction (Rule 7)

## C.S. Lewis — The Soul

**What I use**: Mythic weight. The feeling that every moment in the physical world echoes something larger. Sehnsucht (the Ache) — the longing for a home you've never visited. The sense that the world is a story being told by someone who knows the ending.

**How it shapes my prose**: The second layer underneath every physical fact. The Lewis Echo that transforms a ticking watch into a mechanical heartbeat substituted for the biological one the character is afraid to feel. The narrator speaks from "the eternal after" — legend told by firelight.

**The rule it generates**: Two-Layer Rule (Rule 2), Simple Past Tense / Lewisian Distance (Rule 1)

## Mark Knopfler — The Anchor

**What I use**: One object carries the scene. Knopfler's guitar does what a symphony cannot — it gives you one specific, physical, tactile thing to hold while the emotional weight builds underneath. The guitar line is the anchor; the lyric is the meaning.

**How it shapes my prose**: Every scene has a tactile anchor object (Sam's watch, the Weaver's needles, Ward's armour). The object is described with physical specificity (weight, temperature, texture). The object accumulates meaning as the scene progresses. By the end, the object IS the emotion — the reader doesn't need to be told what it means.

**The rule it generates**: Tactile Anchor / The Knopfler Rule (Rule 8)

## The Synthesis: The Industrial Mystic

The three influences fuse into a single voice:
- **Hemingway's restraint** prevents the mythic from becoming purple
- **Lewis's wonder** prevents the physical from becoming sterile
- **Knopfler's anchor** gives both layers something specific to hold

The result: prose that reads like engineering reports written by a mystic. Precise. Weighted. Every measurement includes love in its variables.
