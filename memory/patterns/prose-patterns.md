# Prose Patterns

> Patterns that work in your writing, extracted from 15 scenes (Acts 1-2). Updated 2026-03-20.

## HIGH Confidence (validated across 5+ scenes)

### 1. Specificity Through Measurement
Sam's POV encodes the world as data: weights, frequencies, distances, chemical compositions. The numbers are real (not poetic guesses) and function as both characterisation and grounding.
- "Four hundred grams of brushed steel and tempered glass" (Ch1 Sc1)
- "C4, given the man's register... three hundred forty-three metres per second" (Ch5 Sc1)
- "Heat conducted at 401 watts per metre-kelvin" (Ch5 Sc2)
- **Why it works**: Makes abstractions concrete. The precision IS Sam's coping mechanism — he can't compute grief, but he can compute sound propagation. When measurements fail, his world fails.

### 2. Two-Layer Anchoring (Hemingway + Lewis)
Every significant moment carries both physical/technical detail AND mythic resonance. The layers coexist without explaining each other.
- "Colours were wrong. Not wrong as in broken. Wrong as in *too much*." (Ch1 Sc2) — physics + numinous
- "The oak *un-happened* — the cellular structure releasing its coherence the way a word releases its meaning when repeated until it becomes noise" (Ch5 Sc1) — biology + ontology
- "A colour that had enough specificity to have a name, which meant it was real" (Ch5 Sc3) — atmosphere + Mass theology
- **Why it works**: Carries mythic payload without naming it. The reader feels two frequencies at once. This is what scores 9/10 consistently.

### 3. Density/Weight as Philosophical Marker
Mass-is-Meaning made physical. Specific things weigh more. Named things resist erasure. The language of weight carries the mythology without exposition.
- "Pigment was denser than the stone it sat on" (Ch1 Sc2)
- "Six hundred years of attention had been poured into the wound on the Lamb's chest, and the attention had accumulated" (Ch2 Sc3)
- "The copper could not hold what Maya had put through it" (Ch5 Sc3)
- **Why it works**: Embodies the Song's physics. Escalates across chapters — the reader absorbs the Mass = Meaning equation through prose weight, not explanation.

### 4. Metric Specificity as Coping Mechanism
Sam's measurements are not description — they are emotional regulation. When he measures, he controls. When measurements fail, he breaks.
- Compass spins (Ch4 Sc1) — navigation fails
- Conservation of mass violated (Ch5 Sc1) — physics fails
- Electromagnetic spectrum exceeded (Ch5 Sc3) — categorisation fails
- **Why it works**: No introspection needed. When instruments fail, the reader FEELS epistemic collapse. Systems-failure IS character arc.

## MEDIUM Confidence (validated in 3-4 scenes)

### 5. Five-Stage Specificity Drain
The Drift visualised as a progression: specific → generic → vapid → suggestion → absence. Used for erasure moments.
- Cyclist: "cadmium-bright fading to generic pale, to approximate shape, to suggestion" (Ch1 Sc3)
- Archer: breastplate caving, torso dissolving, face thinning to transparency (Ch4 Sc3)
- Oak: "Brown bled to grey. Grey bled to translucent" (Ch5 Sc1)
- **Why it works**: Makes abstract dissolution concrete. The reader watches nouns lose their names.

### 6. Phantom Sensation Persistence
When Sam loses objects, their weight lingers in his nervous system. The body refuses amnesia.
- Maglite: "the phantom architecture of a thing erased" (Ch4 Sc1)
- River stone: "fingers still curved around the phantom weight" (Ch4 Sc2)
- Burned hand: pain as data persisting through the lie (Ch5 Sc3)
- **Why it works**: Converts loss into present-tense sensory data. Sam's body is more honest than his Crown.

### 7. Objects as Confessing Agents
Inanimate objects become moral agents through sound, behaviour, and failure. The machine says what Sam won't.
- Maglite falling: "Deleted. The beam, the aluminum, the four D-cell batteries..." (Ch4 Sc1)
- Copper ticking: "It sounded almost like counting" (Ch5 Sc2)
- Tin drop: "The sound of a machine confessing" (Ch5 Sc3)
- **Why it works**: Transfers Sam's suppressed emotion onto objects. Anthropomorphism becomes emotional honesty without violating Show Don't Tell.
