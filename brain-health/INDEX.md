# Brain Health — Growth Tracking

Track how your writing system evolves over time.

## Files

- `growth-log.md` — Timeline of milestones (setup, first scene, first high score, etc.)
- `pattern-confidence.md` — Maturity of extracted patterns (LOW → MEDIUM → HIGH)
- `quality-metrics.md` — Quality score trends across scenes

## Growth Stages

| Stage | Scenes Written | What's Happening |
|-------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Initialized** | 0 | Setup complete, rules defined |
| **Exploring** | 1-5 | Testing voice, calibrating thresholds |
| **Building** | 5-15 | Patterns emerging, voice solidifying |
| **Compounding** | 15-30 | HIGH confidence patterns auto-applied |
| **Mastery** | 30+ | System deeply personalized, consistent quality |

Run `/grow` to see your current dashboard.
