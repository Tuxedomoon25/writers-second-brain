## Best Practices

1. **Run `/setup` first** — it generates your personalized CLAUDE.md, config, and voice profile
2. **Start with characters** — `/character-forge` before `/write-scene`
3. **Start with low thresholds** — set quality gates at 6/10, tighten as you learn
4. **Refine your rules** — after 5-10 scenes, revisit your commandments. What works? What's missing?
5. **Trust the pipeline** — the quality checks catch issues. Focus on the story, not the polish.
