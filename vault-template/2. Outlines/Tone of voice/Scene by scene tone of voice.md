# Scene-by-Scene Tone of Voice

Track how the tone shifts across your story. Not every scene has the same energy.

## Tone Guide

## Track Styles

- **Legato** — Flowing, long sentences, atmospheric descriptions, emotional resonance
- **Staccato** — Short, sharp, clipped. Action scenes, moments of shock or clarity
- **Crescendo** — Building from quiet to loud, paragraph length increasing, tension escalating

## How to Use This

Before writing a scene, check this guide. What emotional key should the reader feel? What rhythm supports that?

After writing, compare what you actually wrote to what you planned. Adjust either the scene or the plan.
